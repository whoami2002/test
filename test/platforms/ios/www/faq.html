FAQ
 
  1. What is Selfie Share?
    Selfie Share is a lively and quirky way to share selfies with friends. Snap a selfie, then say something to add meanings or fun. Friends will hear your narration when viewing it. You are not only taking a photo, but also telling a story.
  2. Could I share photo via other social platforms?
    Yes, you could share your photo on Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, Flickr or Tumblr. In the last stage of uploading a photo, select platform(s) that you would like to post photo on. Then you could post photo to multiple platforms automatically.
  3. Could I forward the photo to Instant Messaging apps like WhatsApp?
    Yes. There is an arrow button in bottom right of each photo. Click that button and share photos with friends via WhatsApp, WeChat or Line.
  4. Who can see my photo?
    All photos are public by default, which means they are visible to anyone using Selfie Share. We would probably add "private" option in the near future.
